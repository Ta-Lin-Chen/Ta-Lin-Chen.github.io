Welcome to my Ark Survival Evolved must-tame dinosaur list!

Acrocanthosaurus

The Acrocanthosaurus is a large theropod that possesses strong power and gets enraged once received enough damage.

Basilosaurus

Basilosaurus is one of the strongest ocean creature which has a huge health bar and immune to torpor damage.

Giganotosaurus

Giganotosaurus is the strongest land carnivore which has the highest damage and one of the highest speed. The only downside is the poor stamina stats which means that it cannot sprint for long.
